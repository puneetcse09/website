Functional Modules(19)

    Attandence Management

    Attendance management system helps school management to improve monitoring and tracking capabilities. The engineered system has been designed by taking inputs from various stakeholders involved in school system. Niograph’s attendance management system provide excellent user friendly GUI, real time attendance and reliable & accurate tracking which is simple and easy to use. The system also allows the users to view the attendance on Android devices. It also helps schools eliminate cost involved in maintaining paper based attendance sheets. Niograph’s system also help the user to generate reports. These reports can be generated for different time intervals showing the trend. These trends can be generated on different parameters like classes, sections and students.

    Library Management

    School Library is at the center of academic development. Whether serving students, teachers, or parents, mission is clear: provide fast and easy access to complete archive of books, videos, and digital media in a cost effective manner. eM’s, Library Management System is 100% Web-based library management system, offers students, faculty and staff a comprehensive, reliable, and easy-to-use system providing instant access from any computer or mobile device 24 hours a day. eM streamlines many of most time-consuming management tasks and empowers students and teachers to manage their own accounts within the circulation rules. Students will enjoy their ability to effortlessly delve into your catalog as well as navigate to educational databases and other on-line resources including ebooks.

    Transport Management

    Transportation is another basic facility, provided by school / colleges to the students, staff and faculty. Thus promoting a healthy habit of commuting using shared resource and saving money and reduce traffic in campus and around the city. This facility gives freedom to parents from dropping their wards daily and thus saving parent’s precious morning time. eM’s transport management focuses on tracking various modes of transportation like buses, vans and small vehicle. Our module provides real time updates and tracks vehicle on standard google map application. This module also helps in generating reports related to travel logs, miles per trip, expenses related to maintenance works

    Alumni Management

    School prepares students to face challenges and obstacles present in the world and opposite is also true. Students are representatives of a school in front of the world. These students are the seeds of progress for school’s future. When these students graduate from school or college, they help school secure vital resources to run it more efficiently without any impediments. eM’s alumni management helps institution maintain the records of these students after they leave school. Our system helps school contact alumnus when there are important events in the school. The system can also send periodic email blast to all with just one click. The system is also another way to help build relationships between people even after they have left the campus. Alumnus are important resource to any school and we in eM help institutions maintain communication with these entities.

    Student Management

    There are variety of operations which happens in a school every day. Majority of these operations involves student in one way or the other. Thus managing records related to students is important part of school operations. eM’s student management helps administrative staff to perform various operations like admission process, schedule preparation for the classes, schedule preparation related to examinations result publication etc. Our product helps generating a wide variety of reports related to student operations like admissions, departures, examinations, activities and resources availability for students. The application tracks various activities in real time thus providing accurate information every time.
